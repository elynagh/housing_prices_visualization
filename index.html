CHAPS: Columbus Housing Analyzing Prices and Sales    

   

        The goal of this project is: Show geographical differences in Columbus housing value at the community level. Our Client would like to see the geographical and temporal differences in changes in housing prices. We have collected data from Columbus REALTORS https://www.columbusrealtors.com/housing-reports/. The visualization interface shows geographical changes in average prices from 2018 to 2021 via heatmap, and temporal changes in average prices and # sales each year from 2016 to 2021 for each location.   Our observations are south and west Columbus had strong housing price growth, northwest and central Columbus had weaker growth, matching the claim that housing trends differ at the community level.
